Planetary Exploration

Exploring planets, both within our solar system and beyond, has been a major focus of space missions. Mars rovers, space telescopes, and planetary probes have provided invaluable data about our neighboring planets and their potential for habitability.

Future missions aim to explore the icy moons of Jupiter and Saturn, where subsurface oceans may harbor life. The study of exoplanets also holds the promise of discovering planets that could resemble Earth.
